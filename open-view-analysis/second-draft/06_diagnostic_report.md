**Method:** Between each major beat, insert either BUT, THEREFORE, or AND THEN. BUT and THEREFORE indicate strong causal binding. AND THEN indicates weak/episodic connection.

**Causal Chain Reconstruction:**

```
[FAMILY DYSFUNCTION ESTABLISHED]
    ↓ THEREFORE
[DAD PROPOSES BABY - creates irony with Mom's secret]
    ↓ BUT
[PARALLEL DESCENTS - Dad, Sis, Son spiral separately]
    ↓ AND THEN (WEAK)
[GAME ANNOUNCED - arbitrary activation]
    ↓ THEREFORE
[DEATHS ESCALATE - systematic elimination]
    ↓ THEREFORE
[MOM KILLS TO SAVE SIS]
    ↓ THEREFORE
[SIS SURVIVES]
    ↓ BUT
[SURVIVAL = CONSCRIPTION INTO NEXT CYCLE]
```

**Causal Binding Score:** 78% of major transitions are causally bound (BUT/THEREFORE)

**Assessment:**
- **60-80%: Adequate binding** — some episodic elements to address

The primary causal weakness is the transition from Movement III (parallel descents) to Movement IV (game). The game's activation appears arbitrary—nothing the characters do causes it. While this arbitrariness may be thematically intentional (violence is systemic, not personal), it creates a structural gap. Consider: does Son's conspiracy have any relationship to the game's activation?

## IV. Identified Issues

### CRITICAL (Structural)

**Issue C1: Game Activation Has No Causal Trigger**

- **Location:** Lines 2281-2337
- **Description:** The death-game activates without apparent cause. Nothing the characters do triggers it; no external force is identified.
- **Evidence:** HOME simply announces rules; no explanation of why now, why this family
- **Impact:** Creates structural gap; feels arbitrary; undermines causal logic
- **Suggested Approach:** Plant information that connects game to one character's action. Options: (1) Son's conspiracy was meant to disrupt the show, and the game is retaliation; (2) Mom's lawyer consultation triggered surveillance escalation; (3) Dad's affair was being filmed, and crossing a line activated the game. Any causal connection would strengthen architecture.
- **Effort Estimate:** Medium

**Issue C2: Movement III Sprawls Without Binding**

- **Location:** Lines 1110-1867 (~20% of script)
- **Description:** Three parallel storylines (Dad's trip, Sis's auditions, Son's conspiracy) proceed with MEANWHILE connections. Internal coherence is adequate; binding to each other and to central throughline is weak.
- **Evidence:** Causal binding score ~55% for this section
- **Impact:** Pacing drag; audience may disengage before game begins
- **Suggested Approach:** (1) Cut 150-200 lines—especially beat 33 (fantasy sequence), one audition beat, one Dad beat; (2) Interleave subplots more tightly rather than sequencing them; (3) Add one scene where subplots intersect before the game (e.g., Sis encounters Dad at airport)
- **Effort Estimate:** High

**Issue C3: Delayed Inciting Incident**

- **Location:** True inciting incident at line 2281 (~60% through script)
- **Description:** The game announcement—which launches the central dramatic question—occurs 60% into the script. Prior material functions as extended setup.
- **Evidence:** Movements I-III are "setup for a show the audience doesn't know they're watching"
- **Impact:** May lose audience before payoff; unconventional structure requires tolerance
- **Suggested Approach:** (1) Accept as formal gambit—this is what the script is; (2) Plant more explicit hints that something is watching/orchestrating; (3) Create a false inciting incident with actual consequences (Dad's proposal currently serves this but has no consequences until game)
- **Effort Estimate:** Medium (if hints) / High (if restructure)

### IMPORTANT (Character/Theme)

**Issue I1: Son's Conspiracy Motivation Unclear**

- **Location:** Lines 1670-1730, throughout
- **Description:** Son and Leader handle explosives and discuss "wiping out a planet," but their actual plan is never clarified. Is it terrorism? Suicide pact? Show disruption?
- **Suggested Approach:** Give Son one scene where he articulates (even obliquely) what the explosives are for. Connect to surveillance theme: "They're always watching. We're going to give them something to watch."

**Issue I2: Dad's Childhood Trauma Revealed Too Late**

- **Location:** Lines 3348-3564 (Movement VI coda)
- **Description:** Dad's sexual assault in childhood explains his adult dysfunction, but this revelation comes after his death and in a section many viewers may find tonally disconnected.
- **Suggested Approach:** Plant earlier—a brief flashback during the hotel assault sequence, triggered by the violence. Let the connection between his victimization and perpetration be visible before the coda explicates it.

**Issue I3: Sis Lacks Active Choice**

- **Location:** Throughout
- **Description:** Sis is consistently reactive—things happen to her (typecast, arrested, trapped, survives). Her one moment of agency (asking "Why should I survive?") is a question, not an action.
- **Suggested Approach:** Give Sis one choice in Movement IV that affects outcome. Example: she could refuse to search for explosives, or she could choose to hide someone. The choice doesn't need to be heroic—it needs to exist.

**Issue I4: Mom/Sis Relationship Underdeveloped**

- **Location:** Movements I-III
- **Description:** The emotional climax (Mom's sacrifice for Sis) depends on their relationship, but they share minimal screen time before the game. Their interactions are distant or transactional.
- **Suggested Approach:** Add one scene in Movement II where Mom and Sis connect authentically—perhaps during the beach day. Sis confides something; Mom responds with genuine care. This seeds the final sacrifice.

**Issue I5: Antagonist Absence**

- **Location:** Lines 1-2280
- **Description:** No personified antagonist appears until HOME speaks. The script's opposition is interpersonal and internal rather than protagonist-vs-antagonist.
- **Suggested Approach:** Either (1) accept as formal choice (art cinema doesn't require antagonist), or (2) introduce one scene suggesting human orchestration—a figure watching monitors, selecting families, activating games. Even indirect reference would provide focus.
