### D. Aristotelian Principles

**Source:** Aristotle, *Poetics* (c. 335 BCE)

| Principle | Application | Assessment |
|-----------|-------------|------------|
| **Unity of Action** | Single through-line: family's destruction/survival | ✓ Present |
| **Unity of Time** | ~48-72 hours (compressed in game) | ✓ Compressed |
| **Unity of Place** | Multiple locations → single location (game) → multiple (coda) | ⚠ Partial |
| **Probability & Necessity** | Game activation lacks causal trigger | ⚠ Concern |
| **Recognition (Anagnorisis)** | TV reveal; Sis-as-Auntie reveal | ✓ Strong |
| **Reversal (Peripeteia)** | Game announcement; survival = conscription | ✓ Strong |
| **Catharsis** | Unclear—script may frustrate rather than purge | ⚠ Concern |
| **Hamartia** | Mom's secret-keeping as tragic flaw? | ⚠ Underdeveloped |

**Key Correspondence:**
OPEN VIEW's strongest Aristotelian elements are its Recognition and Reversal. The game announcement is a classic peripeteia—everything changes in an instant. The coda's revelation (Sis becomes Auntie, family loops eternally) is a second reversal that reframes the apparent resolution.

**Departure:**
Aristotle's requirement for "probability and necessity" challenges the game's activation. In tragedy, catastrophe should flow from character flaw or fate. Here, catastrophe arrives from an external system with no visible trigger. This is post-Aristotelian: the catastrophe is systemic rather than character-driven, reflecting contemporary anxieties about algorithmic violence.

**Catharsis Question:**
Does the script achieve catharsis (emotional purgation through pity and fear)? The ending's cyclical nature may frustrate catharsis—there is no resolution, only recursion. This is deliberate (the script argues against redemption) but may leave audiences in unresolved distress rather than tragic clarity.

## VII. Theoretical Synthesis

### What OPEN VIEW Is Doing Theoretically

OPEN VIEW synthesizes multiple theoretical frameworks into a coherent (if challenging) formal argument:

1. **Structurally:** The script operates in art cinema mode with delayed inciting incident and cyclical resolution, deliberately violating commercial screenplay conventions.

2. **Thematically:** It extends Debord/Foucault surveillance critique into algorithmic content production, arguing that family life under surveillance capitalism is always already entertainment.

3. **Psychologically:** It dramatizes Bowen family systems theory, showing how dysfunction transmits across generations through patterns rather than choices.

4. **Generically:** It hybridizes domestic melodrama, reality TV critique, and survival horror, using genre collision to defamiliarize each mode.

5. **Metatextually:** It implicates viewers as consumers of the very spectacle it depicts, extending reality TV critique to all narrative consumption.

### Theoretical Contribution

OPEN VIEW's theoretical innovation is the combination of **cyclical structure** with **surveillance critique**. Where traditional surveillance narratives end with exposure or escape, OPEN VIEW argues there is no outside—exposure IS the entertainment, and escape IS conscription. This is theoretically sophisticated but demands significant audience tolerance.
